Planning to visit a city: choosing a date, arranging air travel (or other travel), and getting a hotel is the easy part. After that, doing a lot of research online or with various guidebooks to plan out your time in this place. But most of the information you'll find is static; the big tourist spots are always recommended, ignoring the fact that other factors determine the enjoyment of those tourist spots. Visting the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco is usually a must for most travelers, but on a foggy Bay it's not really worth it. But what if there was an app that could help you plan your itinerary around dynamic events like weather, traffic, large events, time of day/year, etc.

Get Around with a Local helps make your visit as memorable and efficent as having a friend in that city. The app will take your desired tourist destinations and will plan your itinerary taking into account weather, best viewing times, transportation conditions, and other dynamic data. Your trip can be as fast or slow paced as you want. At any time, if want to alter your itinerary the app will adapt and re-schedule your events to meet your needs. It's impractical and unrealistic to have each visitor learn how to explore and enjoy a city like a local would, so instead let Get Around with a Local be your friend in this city. Starting with these cities: New York City, New York (all 5 boroughs), and Denver, Colorado
